About Kim Jisoo

Jisoo was born and raised in Korea. Although she was at first treated like an out cast for being 'ugly' in her child years, she was known for her remarkable beauty during her teen years. She joined the drama club when she was 15.Jisoo auditioned for YG entertainment in the year 2011.In 2015, she made a cameo appearance in the KBS2 drama The Producers with label-mates Sandara Park of 2NE1 and Kang Seung-yoon of Winner. Jisoo debuted as one of the four members of Blackpink on August 8, 2016, alongside Jennie, Rosé and Lisa, with the release of their single album Square One.



From 2017 to 2018, Jisoo joined Inkigayo as an MC alongside Got7's Jin-young and NCT's Doyoung. She ventured into acting in 2019 with a short cameo appearance in the tvN fantasy drama Arthdal Chronicles. On August 18, 2020 YG Entertainment confirmed Jisoo would star in her first fixed role as a lead actress in the upcoming JTBC drama Snowdrop alongside actor Jung Hae-in. Notably, through Blackpink's first Korean-language studio album, The Album, Jisoo co-wrote their lead single, "Lovesick Girls".
In December 2019, Jisoo became a local ambassador for Dior's cosmetics brand "Dior Beauty". The next summer, Jisoo was recruited to be Dior's muse and modelled for Dior's Fall/Winter 2020 collection; in September 2020, Jisoo covered the 155th edition 2020 of Dazed Korea, where she discussed her work with Dior. She was also appointed as the first main model for Cartier's digital project in the return of "Pasha de Cartier" for Korean MZ generations. In December 2020, Jisoo was photographed with the Lady Dior and D'Lite bags in Dior's Cruise 2020–2021 Collection. In January 2021, Dior Beauty's "Dior Forever Skin Glow Cushion", endorsed by Jisoo, was released exclusively in Korea.
